About The Certification

Hello, and thank you for looking into the Maryland Travel Counselor Certification process. The requirements to start the program are: -Be an MD State employee for at least a year. -Has not taken the test more than twice in a year. If you meet these requirements, fantastic! You can be a certified travel counselor!

Here's how it works:

Sign up on the home page and enter all your information in. Your information will then be verified and you will be notified if you are accepted into the program.

Next, you will have to complete [insert number here] training modules. Once you complete every module, you will have access to the test! Everything, from the modules to the test, will be available online for you to take at your convenience. If you get a passing score on the exam, you will receive a certication from US Travel
